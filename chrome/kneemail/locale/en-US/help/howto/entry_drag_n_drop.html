Entry Drag and Drop
You can move entries from one folder to another using the "Drag & Drop" method.

First, you select the entries in one folder you want to move. Then, you click and hold onto the mouse button to begin the drag. Next, move your mouse over the folder you wish to move the selected entries to. Finally, let go of the mouse button.

You can select multiple entries if you wish. The first method is to hold down the "Control (Ctrl)" key on your keyboard while clicking on the entries. The second method is to click the first entry in a series, hold down the "Shift" key on your keyboard and then select the last entry in a series.
