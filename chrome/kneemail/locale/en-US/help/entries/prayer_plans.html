Prayer Plans

The following are the ways to launch Prayer Plans:

  • Clicking this toolbar button.

A Prayer Plan is simply a prayer list. It is a way to organize how and what you pray for each day. When you click the toolbar button, you are presented with the Prayer Plan dialog:

After you have prayed for a prayer request, you can click on the checkbox to have it disappear and automatically reschedule for the next time the prayer request should appear. After you have completed praying for a list of prayer requests in a plan, this dialog will automatically take you to the next plan in your Prayer Plans.

To just pray for a single Prayer Plan or to edit your Prayer Plan names, click on the down arrow next to the toolbar button to reveal a list of Prayer Plans and the option to edit.

You can double-click the prayer request to edit it. An example would be if you have an answer to the prayer request.

To remove a prayer request from a Prayer Plan, you can double-click the entry and click on the Prayer Plan Settings button in the Compose dialog that appears.
