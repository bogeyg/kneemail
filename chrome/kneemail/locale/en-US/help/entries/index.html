Entries

Entries are at the heart of Kneemail. They contain a Subject, Text body, Text answer (for Prayer Requests), Scripture, Type and Status.

  • The Subject defines what the entry contains.
  • The Text body contains the main text of any type of entry.
  • The Text answer contains the answer to a Prayer Request.
  • Scripture is any text from the Bible which would help you in your prayer time.
  • The Types are listed below.
  • The Statuses are listed below.

You can have one of three types of entries:

  1. Prayer Request
  2. Praise
  3. General

Entries have a status associated with them. They are:

  1. Active, meaning the entry is still applicable.
  2. Answered, for Prayer Requests only.
  3. Inactive, meaning the entry is no longer applicable.
