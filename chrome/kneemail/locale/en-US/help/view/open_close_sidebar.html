Open/Close Sidebar

The following are the ways to open and close the left sidebar:

  • Clicking these toolbar buttons.
  • Choosing this action from the View menu.
  • Pressing Control+S on the keyboard.

The left sidebar is a context sensitive frame which contains information specific to the right side content. There are times when you would like to close the left sidebar to make more room on the screen for the content on the right. Choosing this action with do just that.
