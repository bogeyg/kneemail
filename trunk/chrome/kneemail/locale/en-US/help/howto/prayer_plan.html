Below is a screenshot of the Prayer Plan dialog:



After you have prayed for a prayer request, you can click on the checkbox to have it disappear and automatically reschedule for the next time the prayer request should appear.

You can double-click the prayer request to edit it. An example would be if you have an answer to the prayer request.

To remove a prayer request from a Prayer Plan, you can double-click the entry and click on the Prayer Plan Settings button in the dialog that appears.
