Prayer Plans
A Prayer Plan is simply a prayer list. It is a way to organize how and what you pray for each day.

You can access your prayer plans by clicking on the down arrow next to the toolbar icon. From the menu, which will appear after you click the icon, you can choose which plan to start with or you can choose to edit your prayer plans. If you click directly on the toolbar icon you will see all prayer requests from all of the Prayer Plans.

The subtopics of this help topic will give you more detail on how to use Prayer Plans.
