Answered Entry

The following are the ways to mark the selected entry as answered:

  • Clicking this toolbar button.
  • Choosing this action from the Entry menu.
  • Choosing this action from the Entry context menu (right-click menu).
  • Pressing Control+A on the keyboard.

When you choose to mark an entry as answered, you are presented with the Compose dialog again and it puts the focus on the answer text box. When you save the entry, the entry will then be marked as answered.
